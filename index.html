Services

We provide IT solutions for your business

Microsoft 365 & Cloud

We design, deploy, and manage Microsoft 365 environments that keep your business connected and productive. From email setup and user management to SharePoint, Teams, and security policies, HCSystems ensures your tenant is configured for reliability, collaboration, and protection.

IT Infrastructure

We build and manage reliable IT infrastructure designed to support your business operations. From server deployments and network configuration to system administration and maintenance, HCSystems ensures your technology runs smoothly, efficiently, and securely.

Security & Monitoring

Protecting your systems and data is a top priority. HCSystems implements strong security practices including system hardening, monitoring, backups, and threat prevention to keep your infrastructure secure and your business protected.

Custom CoPilot Agents

Developed custom AI copilots to automate internal business tasks and support workflows.

Server Virtualization & Infrastructure

Deploy and maintain virtual server environments.

Active Directory Management & Security

Managed Active Directory environments and implemented security measures to protect your network.

About

Our Story

  • 2026

    Our Humble Beginnings

    HCSystems began with a simple idea. Founder and CEO Hunter Bennett, after working in the IT field for several years, saw firsthand that many people and businesses needed help navigating technology. Not from large, impersonal tech corporations — but from someone who valued relationships, integrity, and community. HCSystems was built on those principles, providing reliable technology solutions while treating every client like a partner.

Meet the founder

Hunter Bennett is the founder and CEO of HCSystems. After working in the IT field for several years, he saw a growing need for reliable, trustworthy technology support for small businesses and individuals. Rather than relying on large, impersonal tech companies, Hunter built HCSystems to provide dependable IT solutions while maintaining the values of integrity, family, and personal service.
