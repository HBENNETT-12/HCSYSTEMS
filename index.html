Meet the founder

Hunter Bennett is the founder and CEO of HCSystems. After working in the IT field for several years, he saw a growing need for reliable, trustworthy technology support for small businesses and individuals. Rather than relying on large, impersonal tech companies, Hunter built HCSystems to provide dependable IT solutions while maintaining the values of integrity, family, and personal service.
