Drone Project

Reverse Engineering and Custom Control System

Tools Used:

  • Wireshark
  • Python
  • Arduino IDE
  • Multimeter
  • ESP32
  • Macbook Pro

Step 1: Reverse Engineering

This step involved disassembling the drone to understand its internal components and communication protocols. I documented the layout of the flight controller, motors, and sensors to identify potential areas for improvement.

I figured out that the ip address of the drone is 192.168.169.1 and my laptop is 192.168.1.100 when connected to the same network. Which marks problem #1

I am currently researching how to bypass the network isolation and access the drone's internal network to further analyze its communication protocols and control mechanisms.

Step 2: Custom Control System Development

Based on the insights gained from reverse engineering, I designed and implemented a custom control system to enhance the drone's functionality and performance. This involved creating new software interfaces and optimizing existing hardware configurations.
